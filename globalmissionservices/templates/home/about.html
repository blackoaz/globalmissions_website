Who We Are

Organizational Mission and Vision

Unfulfilled aspirations have caused hardship for many, especially those from economically disadvantaged backgrounds. Societal structures often suppress these dreams instead of fostering an environment that supports them.

As a result, some abandon their aspirations and turn to escapism, which can lead to greater regret.

In response to these challenges, Global Mission Services Kenya (GMS-K) Charitable Trust was established to provide a platform for identifying marginalized and underprivileged children in Kenyan and international communities, aiming to restore purpose through actions anchored in love.

Our Core Values

01

Holistic Empowerment

We address the whole child, recognizing that education is achieved not only through feeding the mind but also by nurturing the body with school meals, ensuring dignity through menstrual care, and providing a safe, healthy environment with clean water and sanitation.

02

Sustainable Impact

Our initiatives are designed for long-term success. This is reflected in our environmental conservation efforts, training local women's groups to produce reusable pads, and building WASH facilities anchored in community ownership and government partnerships for lasting change.

03

Dignity and Inclusion

We believe every child deserves to learn with safety and self-respect. This drives our commitment to gender-sensitive latrines, breaking the silence on menstruation, and ensuring all children, especially the most marginalized, have what they need to thrive in school.

04

Collaborative Partnership

We achieve more together. Our work is built on active coordination with communities, governments, women's groups, and other stakeholders, sharing best practices and leveraging collective expertise to create a unified front for children's welfare.

05

Evidence-Based Action

We are committed to data-driven decisions. Through rigorous Monitoring & Evaluation, needs assessments, and learning from both successes and gaps, we ensure our interventions are effective, accountable, and continuously improved.

Small actions. Big impact. These projects create visible change in West Pokot communities.
